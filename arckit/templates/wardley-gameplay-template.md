### Common Gameplay Anti-Patterns to Avoid

- **Playing too many games at once**: Focus on 2-3 plays maximum; spreading
  effort dilutes impact
- **Ignoring inertia**: Plays that require rapid evolution will stall if
  internal or market inertia is not addressed
- **Misreading evolution stage**: Applying Genesis plays to Commodity components
  (or vice versa) wastes resources
- **Neglecting doctrine**: Gameplay without sound doctrine (Phase I) often
  backfires
- **Static play selection**: Plays that are right today may be wrong in 12
  months — review regularly
